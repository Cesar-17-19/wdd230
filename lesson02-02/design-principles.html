Rule of Thirds

Adobe

adobe.com

On Adobe's website, important sections and content often align with the intersections of the imaginary grid created by the Rule of Thirds. For instance, their hero images often have key elements placed along these lines for visual balance and appeal.

White Space and Clean Design

Apple

apple.com

Apple's website is known for its clean and minimalistic design, making excellent use of white space to highlight products and key information. The ample empty spaces create a sense of elegance and make the content easily digestible for visitors.

PARC: Proximity

Airbnb

airbnb.com

Airbnb's website effectively uses proximity by grouping similar types of accommodations, user reviews, and related information close to each other. This organization enhances the user experience and helps visitors quickly find what they are looking for.
